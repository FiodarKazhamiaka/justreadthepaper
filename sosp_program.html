SOSP 2021 Program

Logistics. SOSP 21 will be conducted virtually. Content will be delivered through Zoom webinars and Slack will be used for discussion. The poster session will happen in Gather town -- see this 2 minute video for an explanation.

Mirrors. SOSP 21 will run in a mirrored fashion. The North America/Europe mirror (Mirror 1) will take place from 8 AM PDT to 5 PM PDT. The Asia/Australia mirror (Mirror 2) will take place from 8 AM China Standard Time to 5 PM China Standard Time.

Videos will be played synchronously in both mirrors, and authors will be available to answer questions live during both mirrors. There will be different instances of the Ask Me Anything session and the Trivia session in each mirror, which will be conducted live. The business meeting will be conducted live at 8 AM PDT on Oct 27th. It will be recorded and played synchronously during the Asia/Australia mirror.
